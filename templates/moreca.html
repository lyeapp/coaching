Chartered accountants are those professionals who are qualified to take on specific activities within the spectrum of accountancy. This includes topics such as auditing financial statements, filing tax returns and financial advising. The professional body that governs this group of accounting professionals is the ICAI (Institute of Chartered Accountants of India) under the Act of parliament. Chartered Accountancy is the one among the best courses to opt for higher education as it is one of the respectful and challenging career fields. CA course syllabus and system is subdivided into three levels such as Foundation level, Intermediate level and Final level. Each aspirant should initially register with the ICAI in order to appear for the entrance exam which is the CA foundation. Course duration of CA

It takes around 4.5 years to complete the CA program/ be qualified as a Chartered Accountant after +2 . The entry-level foundation course involves the quantitative aptitude test, general economics, mercantile laws and accounting. CA entry requirements for the foundation level test conducted by the ICAI are students who had cleared their 12th grade, not necessary to have studied in commerce stream. The foundation course coaching takes around 6 months. The intermediate course has two groups. It is specially designed to improve the knowledge of accounting standards. Duration for the intermediate course coaching comes up to 7 months. After passing Intermediate , the students who have undergone the on-job training period of 3 years called “articleship” with an audit, are eligible to attend CA final exams.

Job opportunities of CA in india

As a CA, you can work in government or public service undertakings as well as the private sector companies. You can work as a finance adviser, managers of finance & also as financial controllers of the particular companies. You will deal with the matters such as taxation, investigation, audit & consulting with the company. A chartered accountant professional has huge possibility in gaining high profile CA jobs in Kerala and anywhere in India and Abroad The average starting salary for a CA profile who is fresher will be around 36-8 Lakhs per annum. A chartered accountant commands the respect in the society and in the organization where he works in. A CA’s salary also increases upon one’s skill set and experience.
